# Community Mourning for Joren

## Context
After the tragic accident that took Joren's life, the community gathers in the central plaza for a memorial. Kaleb, Joren's father, speaks to the gathered crowd, honoring his son's memory and encouraging others to live as fully as Joren did.

## Participants
- **Kaleb** - Joren's father, grieving but composed
- **Community** - Gathered mourners

## Dialogue

*At one of these gatherings, Kaleb spoke to the community, his voice steady but filled with emotion.*

**Kaleb:** "Joren was a light in our lives. He had a boundless spirit and an insatiable curiosity. He taught us all the value of adventure and the importance of living each day to the fullest. Let us honor his memory by continuing to explore, to learn, and to cherish the time we have together."

## Themes
- Grief and mourning
- Community support
- Legacy and memory
- Living fully
