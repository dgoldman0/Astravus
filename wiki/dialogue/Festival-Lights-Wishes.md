# Festival of Lights Wishes

## Context
At the annual Festival of Lights in Lumen's central plaza, the community gathers to celebrate the harmony of nature and technology. As the siblings release lanterns into the sky, each carries a wish for the future.

## Participants
- **Lyra** - Younger sister, excited by the spectacle
- **Kael** - Older brother, dreaming of exploration
- **Calista** - Middle sibling, artistic aspirations

## Dialogue

**Lyra:** "Look at all the lights!"

**Kael:** "It's like the stars came down to join us."

*They walked through the plaza, marveling at the beautiful displays. As the night drew on, they released lanterns into the sky, each carrying a wish for the future.*

**Calista:** "What did you wish for?"

**Kael:** "To explore new worlds. And you?"

**Calista:** "To capture all the beauty we see."

## Themes
- Dreams and aspirations
- Sibling sharing
- Community celebration
- Hope for the future
