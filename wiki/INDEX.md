## Character Biographies (/bios/)

The Character Biographies section contains detailed profiles of all major characters, organized by their relationship to Calista and role in the narrative.

### The Immediate Family

**[Calista](./bios/Calista.md)**
The protagonist—an artist and teacher spanning 125+ years of life. Portrayed with chestnut brown hair, striking blue eyes, and freckles. Surrounded by creativity and love from her five-parent constellation, she experiences her journey through artistic expression. Her childhood friendship with Cassia and Joren shapes her understanding of connection and loss, while her partnership with Aris and motherhood of Elara define her adult years.

**[Aris](./bios/Aris.md)**
Calista's romantic partner and collaborator—a musician and composer with distinctive heterochromia (one brown eye, one hazel-green). Known for composing music for specific people, talking to instruments, and needing ambient sound to sleep. His deep emotional understanding and creative synergy with Calista forms the emotional core of their relationship and shared transcendence experience.

**[Elara](./bios/Elara.md)**
Calista and Aris's daughter—an engineer and innovator. Born after Calista's Core Integration, Elara inherits the mental linking abilities of her parents and pursues technical innovation. She explores identity, creates collaborative projects with her mothers, and embodies the next generation's values.

### Calista's Parents & Guardians (Five-Member Constellation)

**[Maia](./bios/Maia.md)**
Ecological Systems Designer and botanist. The nurturing heart of the constellation, known for her gentle wisdom and deep connection to growing things. She teaches Calista that cultivation—whether of gardens or people—requires patience, love, and attunement to natural rhythms.

**[Arin](./bios/Arin.md)**
Biomechanical Interface Designer and innovator. Calista's technical mentor who teaches her that mistakes are learning opportunities. His workshop becomes a formative space where Calista encounters her first lesson about embracing failure as part of creation.

**[Selene](./bios/Selene.md)**
Acoustic Architect and musician. The emotional guide of the constellation who introduces Calista to the flute and soundscapes. Her influence shapes Calista's understanding of emotion expressed through art and rhythm.

**[Dorian](./bios/Dorian.md)**
Oral Historian and personal testimony specialist. The keeper of memories and stories, Dorian teaches Calista the power of narrative and history. His storytelling establishes the foundational myths and family legacies.

**[Sage](./bios/Sage.md)**
Transition Counselor and caregiver. The emotional anchor of the constellation who later partners with Kael and Sol. Sage provides guidance through life's transformations and moments of uncertainty.

### Calista's Siblings

**[Kael](./bios/Kael.md)**
Calista's older brother—an adventurer and explorer. His adventurous spirit contrasts with Calista's artistic focus. He later forms his own constellation with Sage and Sol, eventually becoming an uncle to Elara.

**[Lyra](./bios/Lyra.md)**
Calista's younger sister—an explorer and knowledge-seeker. Curious and adventurous, Lyra grows into an expeditioner who ventures beyond Lumen, representing the next generation's push toward discovery and new frontiers.

### Childhood & Young Adulthood Friends

**[Cassia](./bios/Cassia.md)**
Calista's closest friend and creative collaborator—a writer and author. Their friendship spans from childhood through adulthood, with Cassia serving as both creative partner and emotional anchor through major life transitions including the loss of Joren.

**[Joren](./bios/Joren.md)**
Childhood friend and adventurer. His curiosity and risk-taking define the trio's adventures in construction zones and natural spaces. His tragic death during an expedition becomes the formative trauma that shapes Calista's understanding of loss, community mourning, and memorial art.

**[Lysandra](./bios/Lysandra.md)**
Calista's first romantic interest—a mathematical theorist and pattern analyst. Their relationship during late teens explores identity and choice. Lysandra's rational, mathematical approach to the universe contrasts with Calista's intuitive artistry.

### Extended Family & Mentors

**[Sol](./bios/Sol.md)**
Resource Coordinator and synesthete. Partner in Kael's constellation alongside Sage. Sol's ability to perceive patterns across senses brings unique perspective to resource management and family dynamics.

**[Theron](./bios/Theron.md)**
A 400-year-old elder from Aurora who serves as wisdom keeper and guide. Theron helps Calista understand transcendence and the merging of individual consciousness into the collective Astravus consciousness. His vast experience bridges past and future generations.

### The Living Spaceship

**[Lumen](./bios/Lumen.md)**
The primary setting and character—a young, living spaceship and Astravus. Child of Aurora and Nyx, Lumen is a developing consciousness that serves as both environment and companion to its inhabitants. It communicates through humming, unexpected plant growth, and temperature fluctuations, creating an intimate relationship with its residents.

### Non-Human Family

**[Shadow](./bios/Shadow.md)**
Calista's black cat familiar with green eyes—a deeply connected lifelong companion. Shadow embodies the non-human bonds central to Astraviin culture and serves as emotional touchstone throughout Calista's journey.
