# Meeting Joren

## Overview
Calista meets Joren during an impromptu adventure through Lumen's construction zones, beginning a friendship defined by energy, mischief, and daring exploration.

---

## Setting

**Location:** Construction zones of Lumen  
**Time Period:** Calista's early childhood (~Age 5-8)  
**Conditions:** Active construction areas with scaffolding, machinery, and unfinished sections

---

## Description

Calista met Joren when he led a group of children on an impromptu adventure through Lumen's construction zones. His tousled blond hair and piercing blue eyes were hard to miss, and his infectious energy drew others into his orbit.

"Come on, let's explore!" he shouted, his laugh echoing through the halls. "Let's see who can find the coolest thing first!" he challenged the group.

"You're on! But I bet I'll find something amazing before you do," Calista responded, feeling the thrill of the chase.

Joren was a bundle of energy and mischief—a sturdy boy whose enthusiasm was impossible to resist. He had a knack for getting into trouble and leading others on thrilling escapades. His laugh was loud and hearty, capable of lifting anyone's spirits.

From this first meeting, Joren became an integral part of Calista's childhood adventures, often leading her, Cassia, and the others into new territories and exciting discoveries.

---

## Importance

### Narrative Significance
- Establishes Joren's character as adventurous leader
- Introduces the dynamic of the three-friend group (Calista, Cassia, Joren)
- Sets up the construction zones as a place of exploration
- Joren's energy contrasts with Cassia's storytelling nature

### Thematic Significance
- Friendship through shared adventure
- The value of courage and spontaneity
- Exploration as a way of understanding one's world
- The different types of friendship: Cassia (creative) vs. Joren (adventurous)

---

## Outcome

Joren becomes one of Calista's closest childhood friends, introducing:
- A spirit of adventure and risk-taking
- Leadership and initiative in the friend group
- Construction zone explorations that expand their knowledge of Lumen
- A playful competitive dynamic

His later death during an expedition profoundly impacts both Calista and the entire community, making these early memories all the more precious.
