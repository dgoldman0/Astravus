# First Love with Lysandra

## Overview
Calista develops her first romantic relationship with Lysandra, a classmate who shares her love for art and music, navigating the joys and uncertainties of young love.

---

## Setting

**Location:** Various locations in Lumen, especially the secret alcove in the garden  
**Time Period:** Late teens  
**Conditions:** A time of personal growth and romantic exploration

---

## Description

Romance began to weave its way into Calista's life as she entered her late teens. She found herself increasingly drawn to Lysandra, a classmate who shared her love for art and music.

### The Connection

Lysandra was talented and insightful, and they quickly bonded over their shared interests. They spent countless hours together:
- Exploring Lumen
- Visiting art installations
- Attending musical performances

Their friendship gradually deepened into something more, and Calista found herself experiencing the thrill and uncertainty of first love.

## Character Details: Lysandra

From CHARACTERS.md:
- Tall and angular with willowy build
- Cool ivory skin with pinkish undertones
- Long, straight black hair falling past shoulders
- Pale gray eyes with silvery quality, often distant
- High cheekbones and narrow chin
- Long, elegant fingers constantly in motion
- Wire-rimmed glasses usually perched on top of head, forgotten

**Quirks:**
- Sees mathematical patterns everywhere
- Writes equations on any available surface when thinking
- Cannot estimate time (loses hours to calculations, perpetually late)
