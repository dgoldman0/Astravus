# Festival of Lights

## Overview
An annual celebration held in Lumen's central plaza, honoring the harmony of nature and technology through lanterns, bioluminescent displays, music, and community gathering.

---

## Setting

**Location:** Lumen's central plaza  
**Time Period:** Annual event (recurring throughout Calista's life)  
**Conditions:** Evening celebration with the plaza illuminated by lanterns and bioluminescent displays

---

## Description

One of the family's favorite celebrations was the Festival of Lights, held annually in the central plaza. The entire community would gather to honor the harmony of nature and technology, illuminating the night with lanterns, bioluminescent displays, and music.

### The Atmosphere

"Look at all the lights!" Lyra exclaimed, her face glowing with excitement.

"It's like the stars came down to join us," Kael added, his eyes reflecting the colorful lights.

The family walked through the plaza, marveling at the beautiful displays and enjoying the festive atmosphere. The air was filled with music, laughter, and the chatter of the community coming together.

### Key Elements

- **Selene's Performance:** She played her harp on a small stage, her melodies mingling with the laughter and chatter of the crowd
- **Maia's Garden:** Featured as a centerpiece, its flowers glowing softly in the night
- **Lantern Release:** As the night drew on, attendees released lanterns into the sky, each carrying a wish for the future

### The Lantern Release

As the family released their lanterns, they shared their hopes:

"What did you wish for?" Calista asked Kael as they watched the lanterns float upwards.

"To explore new worlds," Kael replied with a grin. "And you?"

"To capture all the beauty we see," Calista said, feeling hopeful and inspired.
