# Annual Celebration of Life

## Overview
Each year on the anniversary of Joren's passing, Lumen holds a celebration of life in the central plaza, honoring his memory through stories and community gathering.

---

## Setting

**Location:** Lumen's central plaza  
**Time Period:** Annual event, beginning after Joren's death  
**Conditions:** A community gathering balancing grief and celebration of a life well-lived

---

## Description

The community found ways to commemorate Joren's life beyond the initial mourning period. Each year, on the anniversary of his passing, they held a celebration of life in the central plaza.

### The Gathering

It was a time to:
- Share stories about Joren
- Express continuing grief in a supportive environment
- Remember the vibrant spirit that had touched so many lives
- Reinforce the bonds of community

### Purpose and Effect

The event brought the community together, reinforcing the bonds that made Lumen so special. While the grief never fully disappeared, the annual gathering provided a structured way to honor Joren's memory while celebrating his impact on their lives.

### Kaleb and Soren's Role

Joren's father Kaleb often shared stories of Joren's adventurous spirit during these gatherings, ensuring his memory remained alive within the collective consciousness of Lumen. His mother Soren's art and presence added visual and emotional depth to the commemoration.

---

## Importance

### Narrative Significance
- Creates a recurring ritual of remembrance
- Shows how communities adapt to rare tragedy
- Provides ongoing structure for processing collective grief
- Keeps Joren's memory alive across generations

### Thematic Significance
- Celebration of life rather than only mourning death
- Community rituals as healing mechanisms
- The transformation of grief into celebration
- Memory as a shared community resource

---

## Outcome

### Immediate Effects
- Provides annual closure and connection
- Reinforces community bonds
- Allows ongoing expression of grief in acceptable context
- Keeps Joren present in community memory

### Long-term Effects
- Establishes a tradition that may outlast those who knew Joren directly
- Creates a model for how Lumen might handle future losses
- Ensures new generations learn about Joren through stories
- Provides Calista and Cassia with structured remembrance time

## The Celebration's Character

Unlike typical celebrations, this event balances:
- **Joy:** Celebrating Joren's vibrant life and spirit
- **Sorrow:** Acknowledging the ongoing pain of loss
- **Community:** Coming together to support one another
- **Memory:** Sharing stories to keep Joren alive in collective memory
