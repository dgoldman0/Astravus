# Plant Placement Disagreement

## Context
While arranging new plants in the garden, Kael and Calista have a disagreement about where to place them. Maia steps in to teach them about compromise.

## Participants
- **Kael** - Older brother, has his preference
- **Calista** - Protagonist, has different idea
- **Maia** - Botanist parent, mediator and teacher

## Dialogue

**Kael:** "I think they should go here."

*He pointed to a bright spot.*

**Calista:** "But they'll look better near the pond!"

*Their voices grew louder, attracting Maia's attention. She walked over and placed a hand on each of their shoulders.*

**Maia:** "Both of your ideas have merit. Why don't we try a bit of both? Compromise often leads to the best results."

## Narrative Reflection
They followed her suggestion, and as they worked together, Calista realized that combining their ideas did indeed create a more beautiful arrangement. It was a lesson in cooperation and respect.

## Themes
- Compromise
- Sibling conflict resolution
- Blending perspectives
- Learning cooperation
