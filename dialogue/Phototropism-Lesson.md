# Phototropism Lesson

## Context
A teaching moment in Maia's garden where scientific wonder meets parental guidance. Demonstrates how Calista's parents blend education with everyday activities.

## Participants
- **Maia** - Botanist parent, explains scientific concepts accessibly
- **Calista** - Young Calista, fascinated by natural phenomena

## Dialogue

**Maia:** "Cali, see how this seedling reaches for the light? It's called phototropism. Plants grow towards light to maximize their energy intake."

**Calista:** "It's amazing how they know where to grow."

**Maia:** "Nature is full of wonders."

## Themes
- Scientific curiosity
- Wonder at the natural world
- Educational parenting
- Observation and learning
