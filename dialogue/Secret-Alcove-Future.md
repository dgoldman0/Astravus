# Secret Alcove Future

## Context
In their secret haven—a quiet, secluded alcove in the garden surrounded by flowering vines and glowing lanterns—Lysandra and Calista share their dreams for the future. This conversation captures the intimacy of first love and shared aspirations.

## Participants
- **Lysandra** - Calista's first love, musician and innovator
- **Calista** - Protagonist, young adult exploring her path

## Dialogue

*One evening, as they sat together in the alcove, Lysandra turned to Calista, her eyes reflecting the gentle glow of the lanterns.*

**Lysandra:** "Cali, have you ever thought about what you want to do in the future? Beyond art, I mean."

*Calista took a moment to consider her question, her mind filled with the possibilities that lay ahead.*

**Calista:** "I want to continue creating. But I also want to help others find their own passions. Maybe teach, or lead workshops. What about you?"

*Lysandra smiled, a hint of excitement in her eyes.*

**Lysandra:** "I've been thinking about combining my love for music with technology. Creating instruments that produce new, unique sounds, or even developing new ways for people to experience music."

*Her enthusiasm was infectious, and Calista found herself inspired by her vision.*

**Calista:** "That sounds amazing. I'd love to see what you come up with."
