# First Meeting with Cassia

## Context
At a community gathering, young Calista encounters Cassia for the first time. Cassia is in the middle of telling a story about a mythical creature, immediately captivating Calista with her storytelling gift.

## Participants
- **Cassia** - New friend, imaginative storyteller
- **Calista** - Young protagonist, drawn in by stories

## Dialogue

*Cassia was in the middle of telling a story about a mythical creature, her voice light and musical. Her green eyes sparkled with wonder, and Calista was instantly captivated.*

**Cassia:** "Do you want to join our adventure?"

*From that moment, they were inseparable.*
