# Elara Gender Identity Conversation

## Context
As Elara grows and explores her identity, she approaches her parents to share what feels right to her. This conversation demonstrates the supportive, accepting nature of their family.

## Participants
- **Elara** - Daughter, sharing her truth
- **Aris** - Parent, supportive
- **Calista** - Protagonist, affirming

## Dialogue

*One evening, as they sat together after a day filled with activities, Elara approached them with a thoughtful expression.*

**Elara:** "I've been thinking about who I am and what feels right for me."

*Aris and Calista exchanged a supportive glance.*

**Calista:** "It's important to understand yourself. Have you found something that feels right?"

*Elara nodded.*

**Elara:** "I think 'she' and 'her' feel right to me."

**Aris:** "Then that's what we'll use. It's all about what makes you feel comfortable and authentic."
