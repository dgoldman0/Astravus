# Drifting Apart with Cassia

## Context
As Cassia's writing career takes off, Calista feels the distance growing between them. In the garden, she finally voices her feelings, leading to an honest conversation about changing friendships.

## Participants
- **Calista** - Protagonist, feeling left behind
- **Cassia** - Successful author, busy with career

## Dialogue

*One evening, as they sat together in the garden, Calista finally voiced her feelings.*

**Calista:** "Cassia, I feel like we're drifting apart. You're so busy with your writing, and I miss the way things used to be."

*Cassia looked at her, her eyes filled with understanding.*

**Cassia:** "Cali, I'm sorry if I've made you feel that way. My writing is important to me, but so are you. I promise I'll make more time for us."

## Narrative Reflection
Her words were reassuring, but Calista knew that things would never be quite the same. They were all growing and changing, and their friendships had to adapt to these new realities. It was a bittersweet realization, but one that made her appreciate the moments they did have together even more.

## Themes
- Evolving friendships
- Balancing career and relationships
- Growing apart while staying connected
- Honest communication
