# Breakfast Dreams

## Context
A typical family breakfast around the large, round table in the central room. Kael shares his vivid adventure dreams, captivating his younger sisters and establishing his imaginative, adventurous nature.

## Participants
- **Kael** - Older brother, animated storyteller
- **Lyra** - Younger sister, eager listener
- **Calista** - Middle child, appreciative audience

## Dialogue

**Kael:** "So, I dreamt I was exploring a hidden cave filled with glowing crystals."

**Lyra:** "Were there any creatures in the cave?"

**Kael:** "Of course! There were these small, luminous insects that guided me deeper into the cave."

**Calista:** "Sounds magical."

- Imagination and dreams
- Sibling bonding
- Sense of wonder
