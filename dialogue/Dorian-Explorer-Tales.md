# Dorian Explorer Tales

## Context
Dorian's library serves as a sanctuary of stories and knowledge. His evening storytimes weave tales of ancestors and distant lands, sparking the imagination of all three children.

## Participants
- **Dorian** - Historian parent, captivating storyteller
- **Kael** - Older brother, eager for adventure
- **Calista** - Present as listener

## Dialogue

**Dorian:** "Tonight, we'll hear about the great explorers of our past."

*He held up an ancient map.*

**Kael:** "Can we follow their routes one day?"

**Dorian:** "Perhaps. But first, let's learn their stories."

## Themes
- Heritage and history
- Inspiration through storytelling
- Dreams of exploration
- Passing down knowledge
