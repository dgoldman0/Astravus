### 1.1 LYRA IDENTITY CRISIS - SIBLING VS DAUGHTER

**The Problem:**
Lyra is inconsistently portrayed as two different characters:
- **In CHARACTERS.md & bios/Lyra.md:** Calista's **younger SISTER** (child of the 5-parent constellation with Maia, Arin, Selene, Dorian, Sage)
- **In events/Lyra-Arrival.md:** Calista and Aris's **second DAUGHTER** (born after Elara)
- **In events/INDEX.md:** Listed under "Parenthood" section as "Lyra's Arrival - Second child arrives"
- **In events/Grandparent-Teaching-Moments.md:** Listed as grandchild alongside Elara
- **In events/Lyras-Adventure-Announcement.md:** Lyra announces to "Calista - Parent" and "Aris - Parent"

**Authoritative Source (CHARACTERS.md/bios):**
Lyra is Calista's **younger sister**, part of the original 5-parent constellation. The prototype and TIMELINE.md also confirm this (Lyra is present in "Early Friendships" alongside Kael, and later becomes an explorer).

**Files Requiring Revision:**
1. `events/Lyra-Arrival.md` - **DELETE or completely rewrite** as Lyra's childhood from Calista's POV
2. `events/INDEX.md` - Remove "Lyra's Arrival" from Parenthood section; Lyra should only appear in childhood/early life sections
3. `events/Grandparent-Teaching-Moments.md` - Remove Lyra as "grandchild"; only Elara should be listed as Calista's child
4. `events/Lyras-Adventure-Announcement.md` - Change "Calista - Parent" to "Calista - Sister" (Lyra is announcing to her sister, not her mother)

**Resolution:**
Calista and Aris have **ONE child: Elara**. Lyra is Calista's younger sister who becomes an explorer.

### 1.2 KAEL IDENTITY CRISIS - BROTHER VS PARENT

**The Problem:**
Similar confusion exists with Kael's role:
- **In CHARACTERS.md & bios/Kael.md:** Calista's **older BROTHER** who later forms constellation with Sage and Sol
- **In events/INDEX.md:** Lists "Maia and Kael" as "Calista's parents" and later as "grandparents"
- **In events/Grandparent-Teaching-Moments.md:** "Kael - Grandfather" teaching Elara and Lyra
- **In events/Constellation-Formation.md:** Lists "Maia and Kael - Calista's parents"
- **In events/Elara-First-Breath.md:** "Kael - Grandfather"
- **In events/Kael-Constellation.md:** "Kael, Calista's father, forms constellation..."

**Authoritative Source (CHARACTERS.md/bios):**
- Calista's five PARENTS are: **Maia, Arin, Selene, Dorian, Sage**
- Kael is Calista's **older BROTHER**
- Kael later forms constellation with Sage (one of the original five parents) and Sol

**Files Requiring Revision:**
1. `events/INDEX.md` - Change "Maia and Kael - Calista's parents" to "Maia, Arin, Selene, Dorian, Sage - Calista's parents" in all locations
2. `events/Constellation-Formation.md` - Change grandparents section: Kael is uncle/brother, not parent
3. `events/Elara-First-Breath.md` - Kael is Elara's **uncle**, not grandfather
4. `events/Kael-Constellation.md` - Change "Calista's father" to "Calista's older brother" throughout
5. `events/Grandparent-Teaching-Moments.md` - Kael teaches as **uncle**, not grandfather; five original parents are grandparents
6. All events referencing "Kael" as a parent/grandparent need correction

**Resolution:**
Kael is Calista's older brother. The five parents (Maia, Arin, Selene, Dorian, Sage) become grandparents to Elara. Kael is Elara's uncle.
