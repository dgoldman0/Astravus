## Description

During this period, Calista and her family had the opportunity to visit Aurora, one of Lumen's progenitors, to meet their extended family. The trip was both exciting and nerve-wracking, as it was Calista's first time traveling to another Astravus.

### The Journey

The journey itself was a marvel. The sensation of moving through space was both exhilarating and humbling. Traveling between Astravii, even relatively "close" ones, required significant time and technology.

### Arrival at Aurora

Aurora was awe-inspiring. It felt similar to Lumen, yet distinctly unique:
- The structures were grander
- Technology was seamlessly integrated into every aspect of life
- The scale spoke of Aurora's thousand-year history

### Meeting Extended Family

Meeting their extended family was a profoundly enriching experience. They welcomed the visitors with open arms, sharing stories and traditions that had been passed down through generations. It was fascinating to see the similarities and differences between their lives in Lumen and life in Aurora.
