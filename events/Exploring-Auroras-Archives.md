# Exploring Aurora's Archives

## Overview
The family explores Aurora's ancient archives, where holographic records depict the Astravii's long history, sparking Lyra's fascination with exploration.

---

## Setting

**Location:** Ancient archives within Aurora  
**Time Period:** During the family's visit to Aurora (Calista's late teens)  
**Conditions:** Educational exploration of historical records

---

## Description

One memorable experience during the visit was exploring Aurora's ancient archives, where holographic records depicted the Astravii's long history. The archives were a treasure trove of information spanning over a millennium.

### Lyra's Reaction

Lyra was captivated by the experience, asking endless questions and absorbing every detail.

> **Lyra:** "This is incredible! It's like touching the past and the future at the same time."

### The Experience

The holographic records brought history to life in ways that traditional storytelling could not. The family could:
- See visualizations of ancient events
- Hear recordings of voices from centuries past
- Explore interactive timelines
- Understand the evolution of Astravii culture

For Lyra especially, this experience planted seeds of fascination with Astravii history and culture that would eventually lead to her becoming an explorer.
