# Socioeconomic Canon Review: latest.md

This document reviews the main narrative (latest.md) for consistency with the newly established socioeconomic canon in wiki/worldbuilding/Socioeconomics.md.

---

## Executive Summary

The main narrative is **largely compatible** with the new socioeconomic canon but is **economically sparse**. It focuses on personal, emotional, and familial development rather than depicting economic activity. This is not inherently a problem—the narrative intentionally centers Calista's internal journey—but several **light enhancement opportunities** exist where subtle references could ground the world more richly without disrupting the intimate tone.

No **direct contradictions** with the new canon were found.

### Book III: Family and Maturity (Lines 900-1100)

**Current Economic Content:**
- Constellation formation process includes counseling
- Counselor mentions "finances" and "budgeting"
- "financial stability" discussed during parenthood preparation
- New house creation mentioned
- Art center event for storybook

**Observations:**
This section has the **most economic content** in the narrative:
- Counselor explicitly discusses "finances" and "stability"
- However, **no specific currency names** are used
- **No guild membership** mentioned for Calista or Aris
- The art center is mentioned but its relationship to guilds is unclear

**Key Quote (Lines 920-930):**
> "We'll need to discuss how to manage your finances. It's crucial to ensure stability for your future child."
> "We've been careful with our finances, but combining everything and planning for a child is a whole new level."
> "That's why we have these sessions. We'll cover budgeting, savings plans, and resource allocation."

**Assessment:** This is generic and **compatible** with the canon but could be enriched. The terms "finances," "budgeting," and "savings" are vague—they could refer to Rays (☼), household ledgers, guild standing, or any combination.

**Enhancement Opportunities:**
| Location | Current Text | Suggested Enhancement |
|----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Financial counseling | "manage your finances" | Could specify: "combining your Ray accounts and household ledgers" |
| Art center event | "organized a casual gathering at the art center" | Could clarify relationship to a guild space or community venue |
| New house creation | Generic description | Could reference household establishment as economic unit with its own ledger |

**Priority: HIGH** — This section explicitly addresses economics. Specific currency/guild terminology would strengthen worldbuilding consistency.

## Conclusion

The main narrative's focus on personal development means economic worldbuilding is appropriately sparse. However, the constellation formation scene (Book III) explicitly discusses finances and would benefit from specific currency terminology to maintain consistency with the detailed economic system now established in the wiki.

The marketplace dream in Book IV is the single most natural enhancement point—it literally depicts commerce and could easily incorporate one or two canon details without disrupting the dreamlike quality.

**Overall Assessment:** No revisions strictly required, but 2-3 targeted enhancements would strengthen worldbuilding coherence without altering the narrative's intimate, personal tone.
