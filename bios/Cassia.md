# Cassia

## Basic Information
- **Age:** Same as Calista (125+ years by later narrative)
- **Role in Story:** Calista's closest childhood friend, creative collaborator, adult author
- **Home:** Lumen (the Astravus)
- **Family Background:** Child of Thalia and Lyron
- **Family Familiar:** Implied connection with pets during childhood adventures

## Physical Description
Cassia is a slender girl with long, wavy chestnut hair that seems to have a life of its own, cascading down her back in a cascade of curls. Her eyes are a deep, sparkling green, always filled with a sense of wonder and mischief. She has a light, musical voice perfectly suited for storytelling, drawing listeners in with every word.

## Early Childhood & First Meeting

### First Encounter with Calista
At a community gathering, Cassia was in the middle of telling a story about a mythical creature. Her voice was light and musical, her green eyes sparkling with wonder. 

"Do you want to join our adventure?" she asked Calista.

From that moment forward, they were inseparable. This simple invitation began a lifelong friendship rooted in creativity, imagination, and shared wonder.

### Childhood Characteristics
- Naturally drawn to storytelling and narrative
- Creative imagination that sparks adventure
- Musical quality to voice and presence
- Infectious enthusiasm that draws others in
- Sense of wonder and mischief balanced together

## Joren's Death & Grief

### Impact on Cassia
When Joren dies in the catastrophic accident:
- Cassia and Calista cling to each other
- Shared grief becomes heavy, suffocating presence
- Treehouse, once place of joy and adventure, becomes memorial
- Their friendship deepens through collective sorrow

### Processing Loss
- Cassia processes grief alongside Calista
- They spent hours talking about Joren
- Recounting favorite memories
- Imagining what he would have wanted them to do
- Keeping his spirit alive through remembrance and continued adventure

### Healing & Transformation
As time passes:
- Pain transforms into bittersweet remembrance
- Way to celebrate impact Joren had on their lives
- "Slowly, the pain began to transform into a bittersweet remembrance, a way to celebrate the impact Joren had on our lives"
- "I miss him so much... Do you think he knew how much he meant to us?" Cassia asks
- Calista affirms: "I think he did... He was always so full of life"

## Adult Development

### Career as Author
- Cassia's storytelling passion develops into writing career
- Becomes renowned author within Lumen community
- Stories explore deeper themes:
  - Identity and self-discovery
  - Love in its various forms
  - Shared heritage and collective experience
  - The mysteries of the universe
  - Relationship between humans and Astravii

### Writing Focus
Her work includes:
- Intricate tales exploring relationships between humans and Astravii
- Stories exploring nature of time and perception
- Pieces examining how Astravii experience time differently
- Imaginative speculation about living across centuries
- Observations about rise and fall of civilizations

### Specific Authorial Thoughts
On one occasion, discussing her work with Calista:
- "Have you ever wondered what it's like for the Astravii to experience time? How their perception might differ from ours?"
- Working to capture Astravii's journey through stars in writing
- Trying to imagine living across centuries and seeing patterns Astraviin can't perceive
- Seeking to express the ineffable and transcendent

## Relationship with Calista in Adulthood

### Evolving Friendship
- Both continue to pursue individual creative endeavors
- Growth sometimes creates distance: Cassia becomes busy with writing career
- Calista initially feels they're "drifting apart"
- Cassia's stories become more personal and introspective
- Calista struggles with feeling left behind

### Honest Communication
When Calista voices her feelings:
- "Cassia, I feel like we're drifting apart. You're so busy with your writing, and I miss the way things used to be."
- Cassia responds with understanding and honesty
- "Cali, I'm sorry if I've made you feel that way. My writing is important to me, but so are you. I promise I'll make more time for us."
- Recognition that they're both changing and that friendships must adapt

### Bittersweet Acceptance
Despite reassuring words:
- Calista knows things will never be quite the same
- They're all growing and changing
- Friendships must adapt to new realities
- Changes make them appreciate moments they do share even more
- "It was a bittersweet realization, but one that made me appreciate the moments we did have together even more"

## Later Years & Continued Collaboration

### Mental Connection
After Calista's Core integration:
- They maintain connection through mental links
- Can communicate across distance even when physical meetings are rare

### Quality Over Quantity

- Deep philosophical discussions about identity, existence, cosmos

- Cassia's stories inspire Calista's art

### Creative Synergy

- Calista reaches out mentally: "Cassia, your latest chapter... it's incredible. The way you describe the Astravii's journey through the stars, it's almost like I can see it."
- Cassia responds: "I'm glad it resonated with you. Your art always brings my words to life in ways I never imagined."
